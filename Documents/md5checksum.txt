MD5 Hash Checksum
-------------------------------
Open command prompt
-- 
certutil -hashfile <file> MD5.

Example from PS3 CFW
---------------------------

> certutil -hashfile "C:\Users\condor\Downloads\CFW 4.88.2 Evilnat Cobra 8.3 [CEX]\PS3\UPDATE\PS3UPDAT.PUP" MD5

  MD5 hash of C:\Users\condor\Downloads\CFW 4.88.2 Evilnat Cobra 8.3 [CEX]\PS3\UPDATE\PS3UPDAT.PUP:
  3202971e05140f24e52b36d5c443ad35
  CertUtil: -hashfile command completed successfully.
