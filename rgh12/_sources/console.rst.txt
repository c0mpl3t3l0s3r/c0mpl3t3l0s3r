Connecting to the console
======================

Connecting to the console
Start up J-Runner then move over to the console

Connect the nand r/w wires to your programmer

.. image:: _images/nandrw.jpg

Here is the spi-nand header on the jr programmer in black
Connect power to the motherboard but Do not turn it on

Also connect the usb from your computer to the programmer

If your drivers installed correctly you should see the programmers icon in J-Runner shown below
Same goes for NandX & Xflasher respectively
